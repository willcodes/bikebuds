Let's get started

How many bikes do you need today?

Bike Buds uses a global CityBikes application program interface (API) and Google's native geolocation wizardry to help you locate nearby bikes for rent. Simply input the number of bikes you need and you will be presented a map with every nearby bike station that has enough bikes for rent, along with directions from your current location.

Bicycle icons provided by the Noun Project, and the dancing-man icon was provided by FreePik, flaticon.com.

Coded and designed by Will Difruscio, Erin Wise, and Christina Spencer.
